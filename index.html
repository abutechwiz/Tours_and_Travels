The beauty of Blue Mountain

The Nilgiris district is one of the 38 districts in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Nilgiri is the name given to a range of mountains spread across the borders among the states of Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Kerala. All types of single use plastics are banned in Nilgiris district.The Nilgiri Hills are part of a larger mountain chain known as the Western Ghats.

Doddabetta

The view from the summit is mind-blowing, greeting the eyes with panoramic visions.  Being the highest mountain peak in the Nilgiris, Doddabetta Peak  offers stunning views of the sunrise and the sunset.

Avalanche Lake

Avalanche Lake Ooty makes the perfect setting for a family and adventure package. Avalanche Dam and Lake are some of the finest attractions in Ooty and receive equal love from adventure seekers, nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Mukurthi National Park

Mukurthi National Park is one of the best trekking places in Ooty. The park is part of the Nilgiri Bio Reserve which is India’s first Bio Reserve. There are many rivers and streams running through the park and they are often frequented by the different species of fauna that inhabit the jungle.

Mudhumalai Tiger Reserve

Mudumalai National Park is one of the most critical ecological systems in the country. Needless to say the place is breathtaking and captivating for anyone who appreciates nature.

Pykara

Pykara Lake is formed as a result of the dam and power plant constructed on the river. The greens around, thick forests at the shores and the pines in the backdrop, all create an atmosphere to make you hop on the decks.

Botanical Garden

The Government Botanical Gardens is undoubtedly one of the most sought after tourist attractions not just in Ooty but in the entire Tamil Nadu. It is a famous sightseeing place to visit in Ooty.
